# Staff

Searching for and identifying employees/job postings on LinkedIn can lead to us identifying which technologies, programming languages, and even software applications are being used.

---

## OSINT Techniques

- Search LinkedIn for company employees and their job titles
- Look at job postings to identify tech stack requirements
- Check GitHub for employee contributions and potential code leaks
- Review social media for technology mentions
