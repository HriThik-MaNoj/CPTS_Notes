#### Inband
- Output of both the intended and the new query may be printed directly on the frontend.
- Two types : **Union based** and **Error based**.

#### Blind
- We wont get the output printed on the frontend.
- We may utilize SQL logic to retrive the outut character by character.
- Two types : **Boolean based** and **Time based**.

#### Out-of-band
- We wont have direct accesss to the output whatsoever.
- We'll have to direct the output to a remote location 'ie, DNS record'
